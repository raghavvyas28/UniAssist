Source: Brochure
Category: Fees
School: School of Engineering & Technology (SOET)

The annual tuition fee for programs under the School of Engineering & Technology (SOET) is as follows:

For BTech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), the annual tuition fee is ₹3,18,000 for Indian students and ₹4,54,000 for International/NRI students.

For BTech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) with specialization in Semiconductor Technology, the annual tuition fee is ₹4,00,000 for Indian students and ₹5,71,000 for International/NRI students.

For BCA, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,60,000 for Indian students and ₹2,44,000 for International/NRI students.

For MCA, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,85,000 for Indian students and ₹2,84,000 for International/NRI students.

For MTech programs (various specializations), the annual tuition fee is ₹1,85,000 for Indian students and ₹2,84,000 for International/NRI students.

For PhD programs, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,03,000.
