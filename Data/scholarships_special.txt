The university provides special category scholarships to support specific groups of students.

A Single Girl Child Scholarship offers a 15% tuition fee waiver for single girl students enrolled in undergraduate programs.

NCU Alumni are eligible for a 10% scholarship benefit on tuition fees.

Additionally, a 15% tuition fee concession is available for the wards and spouse of university alumni.

Under the HATCH Scholarship initiative, girls pursuing STEM programs may be eligible for scholarships of up to 100% based on defined criteria.
