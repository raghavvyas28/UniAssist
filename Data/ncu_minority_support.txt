Category: Student Support
Section: Minority Group Support Centre

The university has established a Support Centre for Minority Groups to promote inclusivity and social awareness. The centre provides advice and counselling services related to cultural identity, race, community inclusion, and mental well-being.

The Minority Group Support Centre is located in the main building, Room No. 326 (Third Floor). The initiative aims to create an environment that supports students from diverse ethnic and cultural backgrounds.
