The annual tuition fee for programs under the School of Law (SOL) is as follows:

For the BBA LLB (Hons) 5-Year Integrated program, the annual tuition fee is ₹2,07,000 for Indian students and ₹3,15,000 for International/NRI students.

For the LLB 3-Year program, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,70,000 for Indian students and ₹2,82,000 for International/NRI students.

For the LLM 1-Year program, the annual tuition fee is ₹2,17,000 for Indian students and ₹3,33,000 for International/NRI students.
