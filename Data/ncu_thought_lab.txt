Section: Thought Lab

The university has established a Thought Lab in collaboration with Brahma Kumaris (Education Wing, Rajyoga Education and Research Foundation, Mount Abu, Rajasthan). The Thought Lab promotes meditation and mental well-being practices among students.

Regular meditation sessions are conducted to help reduce stress and improve thought quality. The initiative includes volunteer classes, regular activities, and open electives aimed at enhancing emotional and mental balance.
