Category: Fees
School: School of Management & Liberal Studies (SOMLS)

The annual tuition fee for programs under the School of Management & Liberal Studies (SOMLS) is as follows:

For BCom (Hons), the annual tuition fee is ₹1,80,000 for Indian students and ₹2,64,000 for International/NRI students. For BCom (Hons) in Financial Markets, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,96,700 for Indian students and ₹2,87,000 for International/NRI students.

For BA (Hons) English, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,38,000 for Indian students and ₹3,34,000 for International/NRI students. For BA (Hons) Economics, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,38,000 for Indian students and ₹3,19,000 for International/NRI students. For BA (Hons) Psychology, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,21,500 for Indian students and ₹1,83,000 for International/NRI students.

For MA in English and MA in Psychology, the annual tuition fee is ₹1,60,000 for Indian students and ₹2,79,000 for International/NRI students.

For BBA (General), the annual tuition fee is ₹2,34,000 for Indian students and ₹3,20,000 for International/NRI students. For specialized BBA programs such as Digital Marketing, Business Analytics, and Entrepreneurship & Family Business, the annual tuition fee is ₹2,59,500 for Indian students and ₹3,41,000 for International/NRI students.
