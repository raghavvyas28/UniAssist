Undergraduate students can avail up to 35% tuition fee waiver for programs such as BTech, BCA, BBA, BCom, BA, and BSc based on their 12th grade performance (CBSE or equivalent board).

The university also offers UG National Level Test (NLT)-based scholarships for students who have performed exceptionally well in national-level entrance examinations such as JEE, CUET-UG, CLAT, and other recognized tests.
