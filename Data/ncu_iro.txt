School: The NorthCap University (NCU)

The International Relations Office (IRO) at The NorthCap University (NCU) facilitates global academic engagement through co-teaching, immersion programs, student and faculty mobility, and collaborative research initiatives. Guided by India’s National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 pillars—Digital-First, Innovation, Internationalisation, and Organisational Readiness—NCU has established strategic international partnerships.

The University has signed MoUs with reputed global institutions to promote student exchange programs, faculty exchange, collaborative research, dual degree opportunities, summer internships, cultural exchange, and academic immersion programs.

NCU’s Global Education Partners include The George Washington University, University of Illinois, University of Chester, Santa Clara University, The City College of New York, Salem State University, Binus University, Loyola Marymount University, University of Dayton, University of North Florida, Northern Illinois University, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia, University of East Anglia, University of Regina, QUT, Tsing Hua University, Providence University, Shibaura Institute of Technology, UDF Centro Universitario, Alma Almaty Management University, and Vilnius Tech.
