The Career Services and Alumni Relations(CSAR) is responsible for managing placements, internships, and corporate engagement activities at the university. The centre works to enhance student employability through structured training and professional development initiatives.

CSAR provides services such as employability skills enhancement, industry internships, career counselling, and facilitation of high-quality job placements. It actively collaborates with corporate recruiters and industry experts to bridge the gap between academics and industry expectations.
