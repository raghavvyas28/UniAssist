Category: Campus Facilities
Section: Disabled Access

The university is committed to inclusive education and equal opportunity for specially abled students, staff, faculty, and visitors. To ensure accessibility and ease of movement, the campus provides ramps at major entry and exit points, dedicated car parking spaces, lifts, and separate restroom facilities for specially abled individuals.

The institution complies with the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016 and works toward creating an inclusive environment where barriers to participation are minimized. The university aims to support the full development of human potential and dignity by ensuring equal participation in academic and campus life.
