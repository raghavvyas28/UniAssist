The university provides extensive indoor and outdoor sports facilities. The campus includes a cricket-cum-football ground with floodlights, an athletics track, a multipurpose sports arena, and an amphitheater.

Facilities available include basketball courts, badminton courts, volleyball courts, cricket pitch and ground, gymnasium, sports room with table tennis, carrom boards, chess, and a pickleball court. Students actively participate in intra-university and inter-university tournaments across India and have won trophies in sports such as basketball, football, table tennis, lawn tennis, volleyball, gymnasium, and cricket.
