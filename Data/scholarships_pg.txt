Postgraduate students are eligible for National Level Test (NLT)-based scholarships based on their performance in exams such as GATE, CUET-PG, NIMCET, GMAT, CAT, XAT, SNAP, and other recognized tests.

The university also provides PG Qualifying-Degree-Based Scholarships. These scholarships are awarded based on undergraduate academic performance. To be eligible, students must have a minimum of 60% marks or a CGPA of 6.0 and above in their graduation.
