School: The NorthCap University (NCU)

The NorthCap University (NCU) has a structured governance framework comprising statutory bodies and committees that guide strategic, academic, and administrative decision-making in accordance with regulatory requirements and institutional objectives.

The Governing Body includes senior leadership and external experts such as Mr. Avdhesh Mishra (Chancellor), Prof. Prem Vrat (Pro-Chancellor and Chief Mentor), Prof. Nupur Prakash (Vice-Chancellor), Shri Vineet Garg, IAS (Additional Chief Secretary, Department of Higher Education, Government of Haryana), Mr. Rajesh Marwaha (Chartered Accountant), Mr. Venkatesh Sarvasiddhi (Managing Director, Springer Nature India Pvt. Ltd.), and Commodore Diwakar Tomar NM (Retd.) serving as Registrar and Member-Secretary.

The Board of Management is responsible for operational oversight and institutional planning. Its members include Prof. Nupur Prakash (Vice-Chancellor), Mr. Shiv Saran Mehra (Founder and Member), Dr. Zorawar Daulet Singh (Founder and Member), Shri Vineet Garg, IAS, Mrs. Kavita Mishra (Nominee of Sponsoring Body), Mr. N. K. Dewan (Founder and Member), Prof. Manoj Kumar Gopaliya (Dean, Academics), Dr. Archana Sarma (Head, School of Law), Dr. Rita Chhikara (Head, Computer Science and Engineering), Dr. Deergha Sharma (Head, Management and Commerce), and Commodore Diwakar Tomar NM (Retd.) as Registrar and Member-Secretary.

The Academic Council oversees curriculum development, academic standards, and policy formulation. It includes Prof. Prem Vrat (Pro-Chancellor and Chief Mentor), Prof. Nupur Prakash (Vice-Chancellor), and distinguished academicians such as Prof. S D Joshi (IIT Delhi), Dr. V. G. Hegde (Jawaharlal Nehru University), Prof. Rajesh Chadha (Brookings India Centre), Prof. Kumkum Garg (Retd.), Prof. H. M. Gupta (Retd.), Dr. Pratishtha Pandey (Department of Science and Technology), Dr. Kavita Khanna, Prof. Swaran Ahuja, Dr. Divyabha Vashisth, Dr. Arjun Singh, Dr. Vaishali Sahu, Dr. Bharti Arora, Dr. Amit Sangroya, and Dr. Neha Jain, among others.

In addition to these statutory bodies, NCU has established committees to ensure campus safety, compliance, and student welfare. These include the Anti-Ragging Committee, which implements UGC anti-ragging regulations; the Student Grievance Redressal Cell (SGRC), which addresses student and staff grievances confidentially; and the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC), which prevents and addresses cases of sexual harassment in accordance with national laws.

These governance structures collectively ensure academic integrity, administrative accountability, regulatory compliance, and institutional development at The NorthCap University.
