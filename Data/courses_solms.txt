School: School of Management & Liberal Studies (SOMLS)

The School of Management & Liberal Studies (SOMLS) offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in commerce, management, humanities, and social sciences.

The school offers BBA programs including BBA (General), BBA in Digital Marketing, Business Analytics, and Entrepreneurship & Family Business. It also offers BCom (Hons) and BCom (Hons) in Financial Markets.

In the humanities domain, the school offers BA (Hons) in Psychology, Economics, and English. Postgraduate programs include MA in English and MA in Psychology with specializations in Clinical Psychology, Organisational Behaviour, and Applied Psychology. PhD programs are also available.
