Source: University Website
Category: Campus Facilities
Section: Hostel

The university provides hostel accommodation for students with limited available seats. The hostel offers furnished rooms with facilities including 24-hour power backup, treated water supply, Wi-Fi, intercom connectivity, indoor sports, and recreational amenities.

The hostel provides hygienic dining facilities and round-the-clock security. A bus service operates between the hostel and the university campus for student convenience. Students who are unable to secure hostel accommodation may opt for alternative lodging nearby.
