School: School of Law (SOL)

The School of Law (SOL) offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in legal studies. The programs include BBA LLB (Hons) 5-Year Integrated program, LLB 3-Year program, and LLM 1-Year program with specializations in Corporate Law, Human Rights Law, and Intellectual Property Rights. PhD programs are also available in law.
